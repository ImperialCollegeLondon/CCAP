- 100 x 140 x 160 mm tank filled with water
- 15 mm thick aluminium walls
- Circular entrance window (15 mm diameters) located 4 mm from the detector center & made of Kapton (50 μm thickness)

- 4 scintillating fibre stations at 0, 1, 2 & 3 mm from the detector center
- Made of 33 scintillating fibres each, 300 μm centre-to-centre distance, 50 μm gap
